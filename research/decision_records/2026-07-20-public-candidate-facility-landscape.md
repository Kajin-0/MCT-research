# Public candidate-facility landscape decision

**Date:** 2026-07-20  
**Issue:** #163  
**Parent feasibility package:** PR #162

## Decision

Official public webpages identify plausible candidates for all seven external capability classes, but confirm no role or feasibility gate.

## First contact sequence

1. **UIC Microphysics Laboratory — material provider.** UIC publicly identifies current university HgCdTe MBE research and a HgCdTe growth/characterization lineage. Specimen availability, target states, uncertainty, rights, cost, and access remain unknown.
2. **National MagLab — absorption and magneto-optics.** Public capability includes IR transmission/reflectance from `10-6000 cm^-1`, better than `0.03 cm^-1` resolution, high magnetic fields, and `4.2-8 K` configurations. A complete 6 K/300 K same-area workflow remains unverified. The next listed proposal deadline is `2026-11-13`.
3. **Washington State University PAS — vacancy proxy.** Public capability includes depth-resolved vacancy-sensitive PAS and CdTe/CdZnTe experience. HgCdTe-specific calibration, uncertainty, temperature transfer, access, and data release remain unverified.
4. **UT Austin E-beam Lab — composition.** Public capability includes external-academic WDS EPMA and high analytical precision under suitable conditions. HgCdTe `sigma_x <= 0.0015` is not demonstrated. Polishing, coating, vacuum, and beam-heating requirements create state-alteration risk.
5. **UT Knoxville Electromagnetic Properties Lab — Hall/state.** Public capability includes external PPMS Hall measurement to `1.8 K`. HgCdTe contacts, uncertainty, state drift, native-data release, and exact temperature control remain unverified.

Backup routes are NC State PAS and the UIC PPMS. Zenodo is a possible archive infrastructure, not an accountable archive owner.

## Authorized

- Public candidate landscape and contact priority.
- All seven external role classes have at least one candidate.
- Role-specific questions are frozen before outreach.
- The MagLab deadline may be used as a proposal-planning date.

## Blocked

- Facility endorsement.
- Role or gate confirmation.
- Assumed access, willingness, cost, schedule, specimen acceptance, uncertainty, rights, or data release.
- Converting generic EPMA precision into HgCdTe composition uncertainty.
- Converting CdTe/CdZnTe PAS experience into Hg-vacancy calibration.
- Extrapolating low-temperature MagLab capability into the complete paired protocol.

## Program consequence

The PR #162 feasibility template remains unchanged and audits `not_ready`. The next step is documented outreach to the five priority candidates. A written response may move a candidate to `conditional`; only the complete feasibility contract can promote a role to `confirmed`.
