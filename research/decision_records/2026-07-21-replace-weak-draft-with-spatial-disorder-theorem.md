## Quantitative screen

For the declared two-dimensional sensitivity case

```text
point composition sigma = 0.005
correlation length       = 5 um
local |dEg/dx|           = 1.7191085 eV
```

increasing Gaussian probe sigma from `1 um` to `100 um` changes the apparent linearized gap standard deviation from `8.271 meV` to `0.304 meV`, a factor of `27.2336`.

Around a `10 um` cutoff, the corresponding first-order cutoff standard deviation changes from `0.6671 um` to `0.02450 um`.

This is large enough to affect comparisons among micro-FTIR, wafer FTIR, PL, cutoff mapping, and pixel/device distributions. It remains a declared sensitivity result, not a specimen calibration.
