## Scope reviewed

The reviewed increment adds only numerical verification around the previously accepted dimensionless steady unipolar finite-volume residual:

- residual-decreasing damped Newton iteration;
- Armijo-style backtracking;
- explicit block residual and step diagnostics;
- explicit controlled termination reasons;
- right-contact voltage continuation;
- continuous manufactured Poisson and continuity forcing;
- mesh-refinement and observed-order analysis;
- dependency-free coordinate storage for dense-Jacobian equality checks.

It does not add a material-accurate HgCdTe model, bipolar transport, traps, recombination, optical generation, dynamic contacts, stochastic operators, or terminal PSD calculations.

## Accepted nonlinear behavior

The solver uses

\[
J(u_k)\Delta u_k=-R(u_k)
\]

and accepts

\[
u_{k+1}=u_k+\alpha_k\Delta u_k
\]

only when the residual two-norm satisfies the declared sufficient-decrease test.

Trial states producing overflow, invalid positive-density reconstruction, or non-finite residuals are rejected rather than clipped.

The solver records:

- full residual infinity norm before and after each accepted step;
- Poisson and continuity block norms;
- step infinity norm;
- accepted damping;
- backtrack count;
- dense Jacobian condition estimate;
- explicit termination reason.

## Manufactured solution

The continuous manufactured state is

\[
\psi(x)=ax+b\sin(\pi x/L),
\]

\[
N(x)=\exp[c\sin(\pi x/L)].
\]

Forcing is obtained analytically from

\[
R_\psi=\psi''-\Lambda^2(N-N_B),
\]

\[
j=d_n(N'-N\psi'),
\qquad
R_n=j'.
\]

The source is not generated by applying the discrete operator to the sampled exact state. Therefore the refinement study tests discretization error rather than only algebraic self-consistency.

## Local evidence

The focused nonlinear test module reports 16 passing tests locally.

Verified locally:

1. exact equilibrium terminates with zero Newton steps;
2. perturbed equilibrium converges to the exact state;
3. a difficult initial state triggers damping and converges;
4. insufficient backtracking reports `line_search_failed` without a non-finite state;
5. voltage continuation recovers the exact uniform-resistor solution;
6. source-free terminal current is spatially constant;
7. manufactured potential and density errors converge at approximately second order on 16, 32, and 64 interval meshes;
8. coordinate-to-dense Jacobian reconstruction is exactly equal to the validated dense Jacobian.

Observed local manufactured `L_inf` orders are approximately:

- potential: `1.998`, `1.999`;
- density: `1.999`, `2.000`.

These values are numerical verification results for the dimensionless reference problem, not HgCdTe predictions.

## Sparse-storage boundary

The current COO path serializes the already validated dense Jacobian and reconstructs it exactly. It verifies storage semantics only.

It is not:

- an independent sparse derivative assembly;
- a sparse direct or iterative solve;
- evidence that sparse and dense nonlinear trajectories are identical.

An independent sparse assembly remains a later gate.
