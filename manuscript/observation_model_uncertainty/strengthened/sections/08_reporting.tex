\section{8. Reporting recommendations}\label{reporting-recommendations}

A defensible HgCdTe optical-edge report should include:

\begin{enumerate}
\def\labelenumi{\arabic{enumi}.}
\tightlist
\item
  the native or legally reproducible source spectrum;
\item
  whether the plotted quantity is directly measured or model-derived;
\item
  specimen identity, temperature, thickness, and optical geometry;
\item
  composition value, method, and uncertainty;
\item
  carrier and processing state;
\item
  the exact reported estimand;
\item
  extraction equation and fitted parameters;
\item
  fit-domain endpoints and membership rule;
\item
  point-weighting and resampling convention;
\item
  baseline and tail treatment;
\item
  parameter bounds and boundary-hit policy;
\item
  model residual diagnostics;
\item
  coordinate-reconstruction and calibration sensitivity;
\item
  alternative admissible extraction models;
\item
  a clear separation between fitted intercepts and fixed-\(\alpha\)
  operational coordinates.
\end{enumerate}
