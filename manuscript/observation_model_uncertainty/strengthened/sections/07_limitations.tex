\section{7. Limitations}\label{limitations}

\begin{enumerate}
\def\labelenumi{\arabic{enumi}.}
\tightlist
\item
  Only two 300 K curves from one publication are analyzed.
\item
  The curves are reconstructed from embedded bitmaps rather than native
  exports.
\item
  The source absorption coefficients are outputs of an optical
  inversion, not direct raw observables.
\item
  Source inversion covariance, thickness covariance, and interface-model
  uncertainty are unavailable.
\item
  Specimen-level composition uncertainty is unavailable.
\item
  Carrier type and carrier density are unavailable.
\item
  The extraction-model family is controlled but not exhaustive.
\item
  Reconstruction and fit-domain sensitivity are deterministic scenario
  analyses, not probability models.
\item
  Boundary-limited candidates are retained only for completeness.
\item
  No conclusion is generalized to other specimens, temperatures, growth
  methods, laboratories, or native-data analyses.
\end{enumerate}
